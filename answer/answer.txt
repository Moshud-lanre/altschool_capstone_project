
			Report on the transformation carried out on Olist ecommerce Data

Transformation was carried out on the olist data and the following models were developed to answer the analytical questions:
1. Sales by category model
2. Average delivery time model
3. Orders by state model.

From these models, the following questions were answered:

1. Which product category have the highest sales?

Answer: The product with the highest sales is beleza_saude

2. What is the average delivery time?
 Answer: The average delivery time is 12 days(288 hours)

3. Which state has the highest numbers of order?
Answer: Sao Paulo (SP)
